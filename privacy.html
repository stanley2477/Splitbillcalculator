Privacy Policy

At Split Bill Calculator, user privacy is safeguarded as one of our highest operational parameters. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we process and how we maintain a secure environment for our users.

1. Data Collection and Processing

Our calculation engine operates entirely client-side right inside your local web browser session. We never read, collect, save, transmit, or synchronize any personal financial variables, individual metrics, or numeric values inputted into our utility forms. Your calculation data disappears immediately when you close or refresh your web browser window.

2. Log Files

Like many other websites, Split Bill Calculator makes use of log files. These files merely log visitors to the site—usually a standard procedure for hosting companies and a part of hosting services' analytics. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and optionally the number of clicks. This data is not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

3. Cookies and Web Beacons

We use cookies to store information about visitors' preferences, to record user-specific information on which pages the site visitor accesses or visits, and to personalize or customize our web page content based upon visitors' browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

4. Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of the third-party vendors on our site. It uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet. Visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy.

5. Third-Party Advertising Partners

Third-party ad servers or ad networks (including Google AdSense and Adsterra) use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons in their respective advertisements and links that appear on Split Bill Calculator, which are sent directly to users' browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These technologies are used to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see on websites.

Please note that Split Bill Calculator has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

6. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
